# Personal Roadmap - Your IBO Success Guide

Welcome! This is your personal roadmap tracker—a dedicated space to help you organize, track, and achieve your goals as an Independent Business Owner.

## What Can You Track Here?

### 📚 Books
Keep track of books you're reading or want to read. Build your knowledge and stay inspired on your entrepreneurial journey.

### 🎯 Habits
Monitor and build the daily habits that drive your success. Track consistency and watch yourself grow one day at a time.

### 📍 Phases
Organize your business goals and projects into distinct phases. Break down big dreams into manageable milestones.

### 🏃 Core Run
Track your core activities and measure your progress. Stay focused on what matters most to your business.

## How to Get Started

1. **Visit the Website** - Open the application in your browser
2. **Choose Your Section** - Start with Books, Habits, Phases, or Core Run
3. **Add Your Goals** - Begin tracking what's important to you
4. **Check Your Progress** - Return regularly to monitor your growth

## Why Use This Tool?

- **Stay Organized** - Keep all your goals and progress in one place
- **Build Consistency** - Track daily habits and stay accountable
- **Measure Success** - See your progress over time
- **Stay Focused** - Keep your most important objectives visible
